Travel texts are simply descriptions of destinations that appear
in travel-related media such as magazines, websites, or brochures.
They provide information about a particular destination, including
its features and highlights. In contrast, destination
texts are written by tourist organizations for their own website
or publication. These texts typically include
more detailed information about a destination than
what’s found in travel texts.
